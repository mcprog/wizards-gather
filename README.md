# Wizards Gather

A roguelike platform sidescroller where you play as an aging wizard who recruits young wizards to fight for you on a quest to reach the Fountain of Youth.

## Game Overview

- **Genre:** Roguelike, Platformer, Sidescroller  
- **Player Goal:** Guide your wizard through 6 stages of enemies, traps, and bosses to reach the Fountain of Youth.  
- **Core Mechanic:** Collect and upgrade minions that fight automatically while you navigate platforms and avoid damage.

## Features

- **Minion System:** Recruit and upgrade unique minions (Mage, Cleric, Shifter, Dwarf, Occultist, Bard, Monk, Templar, High Elf).  
- **Roguelike Progression:** Each run consists of 6 stages with a shop between stages to buy and upgrade minions.  
- **Boss Fights:** Challenging bosses with unique mechanics at stages 3 and 6.  
- **Status Effects & Synergies:** Utilize buffs, debuffs, and combos like BURN + CRIT or EXECUTE.  
- **Favors:** Powerful one-stage bonuses with trade-offs.  
- **Procedural Elements:** Random minion offerings, enemy scaling, and stage generation.

## Controls & Gameplay

- The player controls the elderly wizard directly, using movement and jumping to navigate.  
- Minions orbit the player and auto-attack enemies using mana-based abilities.  
- The wizard cannot attack—survival depends on minion management and positioning.  
- Game ends if the wizard’s health reaches zero.
